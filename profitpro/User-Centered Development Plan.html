Your 3-Stage Journey

Think of this as your roadmap from "where we are today" to "where we'll be" - with clear milestones you can see and experience. Each stage builds on the previous one, so you'll never lose functionality as we add new features.

Stage 1: Foundation Building (Weeks 1-4)

What this means for you: We're setting up the groundwork. You won't see a working system yet, but we'll be asking for your input to ensure we build exactly what you need.

Stage 2: Your MVP Goes Live (Weeks 5-8)

What this means for you: You'll start using the core system. We'll train you and your team, and you'll be able to manage real projects through the platform.

What this means for you: Based on your feedback, we'll fine-tune everything and add the finishing touches that make your daily work even easier.

Stage 1: Foundation Building - "Getting to Know You"

Timeline: 4 weeks | Your involvement: 2-3 hours per week

What you'll experience: This is our discovery phase. We'll interview you, show you mockups, and make sure we understand exactly how you work today so we can build the perfect system for tomorrow.

Week 1: Understanding Your World

For All Users:

  • 30-minute interview: We'll ask about your current process, pain points, and what would make your life easier
  • Process mapping session: You'll walk us through a typical project from start to finish
  • Document review: Show us your current forms, checklists, and approval processes

Specific User Activities:

Contractors: Show us how you currently submit claims and track project progress

Inspectors: Demonstrate your current inspection process and reporting methods

Consultants: Walk us through how you review and approve contractor submissions

Client Reps: Explain your approval workflows and reporting needs

Week 2: Designing Your Experience

What you'll see:

  • Interactive wireframes: Clickable mockups of your future system
  • User journey maps: Visual flows showing exactly how you'll complete key tasks
  • Feedback sessions: 15-minute reviews where you tell us what works and what doesn't

Your role:

Be honest! If something feels confusing or takes too many clicks, tell us immediately. This is your chance to shape the system before we build it.

Week 3: Behind the Scenes Setup

What happens:

  • Security setup: We configure your login system and permissions
  • Data structure creation: We build the foundation for storing your projects and documents
  • Integration planning: We plan how your system will connect with existing tools

Your involvement:

Minimal - we'll send you a 5-minute form to confirm user roles and access levels. Otherwise, we work while you focus on your regular business.

Week 4: Final Preparations

What you'll do:

  • User acceptance criteria review: 30-minute session to confirm we've understood your needs correctly
  • Training schedule planning: We'll book your team's training sessions for Stage 2
  • Test data preparation: We'll ask for 2-3 sample projects to use during testing
Discovery Complete Design Approved Ready to Build

Stage 2: Your MVP Goes Live - "Welcome to Your New System"

Timeline: 4 weeks | Your involvement: 4-6 hours in week 1, then 1-2 hours per week

What you'll experience: This is the exciting part! You'll start using the actual system with real projects. We'll be right there with you, providing training and support as you learn your new workflows.

Week 5: Training & Soft Launch

Day 1: System Introduction (2 hours)

  • Welcome session: Live demo of your new system
  • Account setup: We'll help you log in and configure your profile
  • Navigation tour: Guided walkthrough of key features

Days 2-3: Role-Specific Training (1 hour each)

Contractors: Learn to create projects, upload documents, and submit claims

Inspectors: Master inspection scheduling, mobile checklists, and status updates

Consultants: Practice reviewing claims, adding assessments, and managing approvals

Client Reps: Understand dashboard views, approval workflows, and reporting

Days 4-5: Practice Mode

  • Test projects: Work with sample data to practice without pressure
  • Support chat: Instant help available during business hours
  • Feedback collection: Quick daily check-ins to address any confusion

Week 6: First Real Project

What you'll do:

  • Project setup: Create your first real project in the system
  • Live support: We'll shadow your first few interactions to ensure smooth sailing
  • Daily standups: 15-minute check-ins to resolve any issues immediately

Success metrics:

  • Project created in under 5 minutes
  • First claim submitted successfully
  • All stakeholders can access their relevant information

Week 7: Full Team Adoption

Expansion activities:

  • Additional user onboarding: Bring the rest of your team on board
  • Process refinement: Based on your feedback, we fine-tune workflows
  • Advanced features introduction: Show you time-saving shortcuts and tips

Your wins this week:

  • Complete project lifecycle managed in system
  • First successful payment triggered by inspection pass
  • Team comfortable with daily system use

Week 8: Go-Live Celebration

Final validation:

  • End-to-end testing: Complete project from setup to payment
  • User satisfaction survey: Rate your experience and suggest improvements
  • Success documentation: We'll create quick reference guides for your team

What you'll have achieved:

  • Reduced project setup time from hours to minutes
  • Eliminated paper-based tracking
  • Real-time visibility for all stakeholders
  • Faster payment processing

Stage 3: Perfecting Your Experience - "Making It Even Better"

Timeline: Ongoing | Your involvement: 30 minutes per month for feedback

What you'll experience: Now that you're using the system daily, we'll become your optimization partner. Based on your actual usage patterns and feedback, we'll continuously improve and add features that save you even more time.

Monthly Optimization Cycle

Week 1: Usage Analysis

  • Automated reports: We'll send you insights on how your team is using the system
  • Time savings tracking: Quantify the efficiency gains you're experiencing
  • Bottleneck identification: Spot any processes that could be smoother

Week 2: Feedback Collection

  • Quick pulse survey: 5-minute monthly check-in on satisfaction and suggestions
  • User interviews: Optional 15-minute calls with power users for deeper insights
  • Feature request review: Prioritize new features based on your needs

Week 3: Enhancement Planning

  • Roadmap sharing: We'll show you what's coming next based on your feedback
  • Impact estimation: Explain how new features will save you additional time
  • Timeline confirmation: Set expectations for when improvements will arrive

Week 4: Implementation & Testing

  • Silent updates: Most improvements happen automatically without disrupting your work
  • New feature rollouts: Optional training for significant new capabilities
  • Success measurement: Track the impact of recent improvements
